Integrations

Before you can use Monitr, you will have to set up an integration to connect to your accounting software.
To setup your integration, navigate to  Integrations in the sidebar.

Add an integration#

Click on the accountancy software integration that you use for your company, and you wish to connect to Monitr. If it's not available yet you can always use CSV files to upload your general ledger.

  • Yuki
  • Exact Online
  • CSV File

You will need to create a Yuki API key to be able to add the integration. You can find more information on the documentation page of Yuki. Once you've created an API key, fill it in and click .

If you have multiple entities connected to your credentials, Monitr will show you an entity selection screen where you can decide which you want to import and use in Monitr. If you change your mind in the future you can always enable or disable entities in the  Entities settings.

Once you've added your integration, we will automatically start importing all required data from your accounting provider in the background.

Data synchronisation#

Monitr will get data from your accounts, analytical accounts and general ledger each night. This way your dashboard will always give you an up to date view on your accounts. The data will be updated from the last actuals date that you can set in the  Entities settings. If data prior to the last actuals needs to be updated, change the last actuals to an earlier date, the data will refresh overnight. Set your last actuals to the correct date, after the data has been refreshed.

In addition, you can sync your data manually by clicking the button in the  Entities settings.

Once you save the Entity name, the app will ask you to redirect you to the Entity settings page and import CSV's from there.


CSV Import#

In your Entity card you will see an button, click it, and this will open the Import CSV wizard.

Importing your CSV file consists out of 3 steps:

  1. Select your file
  2. Decide if you would like to overwrite (delete your current general ledger in Monitr) or append to your general ledger and what you use as a decimal separator
  3. Map your columns onto our database structure. To make sure we have the correct information, the first 5 columns are required.

In case something is wrong with your file the app will show an error message on the issue.

End the process by clicking on the button. Monitr will take over from here and keep you updated on the process of downloading and parsing your file.
