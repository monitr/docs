Reporting

How do you define your reporting?#

In Monitr you can tailor your reporting to your need either by using our user interface, or by uploading CSV files containing pre-defined reports. The setup of your reporting can be done here in the tab 'Reporting' .

You can use our google sheets template in order to save time.



What reports do you need to define?#

Your reporting is composed out of 3 reports:

  1. Cash flow statement (tab 'Cash Flow')
  2. Profit and Loss statement (tab 'Profit & Loss')
  3. Balance sheet (tab 'Balance sheet')
