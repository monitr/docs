Budgets

You can manage different versions of your budget via Monitr (see Budget Versions) We recommend to upload your budget as a CSV file. But you can also manage your budget mannually.

To upload your budget:

  1. Go to tab 'Budget'.
  2. Click
  3. A window will pop-up at the right side of your screen
  4. Select the budget version for wich you want to upload your budget
  5. Click
  6. Make sure the field Budget Account is completed for all rows. If this is not the case the Budget Account in your CSV-file was incorrect.
  7. Click
