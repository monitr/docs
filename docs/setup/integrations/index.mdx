Before you can use Monitr, you will have to set up an integration to connect to your accounting software.  
To setup your integration, navigate to [<Icon iconName="PlugConnected" /> Integrations](https://app.monitr.be/dashboard/manage/integrations) in the sidebar.

<BrowserFrame>
  <img src={integrationView} />
</BrowserFrame>

## Add an integration

Click <PrimaryButton text="Add" /> on the accountancy software integration that you use for your company, and you wish to connect to Monitr. If it's not available yet you can always use CSV files to upload your general ledger.
