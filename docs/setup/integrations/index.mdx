Before you can use Monitr, you will have to set up an integration to connect to your accounting software.  
To setup your integration, navigate to [<Icon iconName="PlugConnected" /> Integrations](https://app.monitr.be/dashboard/manage/integrations) in the sidebar.

## Add an integration

Click <ActionButton iconProps={{ iconName: 'Add' }}>Add</ActionButton> at the top of the page and select your accounting software provider.
