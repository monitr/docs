You can either upload a pre-defined template (such as this file) or use tabsheet 'Budget Accounts' our [google sheets template](./template) to define your Cash flow statement.

## Definition
Complete the folowing fields in the template
- Name [string]: This is a unique name
- Mapping [string]: The Account Mapping Name of the Mapping to which the Budget Account is linked. Used to link the Budgets to the Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss statement.
 
## Validation 
Make sure these validations rules are respected:   
<Icon iconName="SkypeCheck"/> The Name of the Budget Account should be unique <br />
<Icon iconName="SkypeCheck"/> A mapping to a reporting line needs to be made in Reporting ID <br />


## Upload your template
1. Make sure the status of all validation rules in the tabsheet 'Budget Accounts' of the template is 'TRUE'.
1. Copy the definition (columns with a dark blue header) in to a csv file and save it on your computer.
1. [Go to tab 'Reporting' - 'Budget Accounts'](https://app.monitr.be/dashboard/organisations/entities/definitions/budget/accounts).
1. Click <ActionButton>Upload</ActionButton>
1. Browse to the CSV file on your computer and select and click 'Open'.
1. Click <PrimaryButton>Save</PrimaryButton>
1. If any errors occur they will be highlighted in red. Your report will not be saved.
1. Fix the errors and click <PrimaryButton>Save</PrimaryButton> untill the report is succelfully saved
